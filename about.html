Co-Founder & Chief Medical Officer

Dr. Bandy X. Lee, M.D.

Forensic & Social Psychiatrist | Public Health Advocate

Dr. Bandy X. Lee is a world-renowned forensic and social psychiatrist and one of the most internationally recognized experts on violence and mental health in the United States. With more than 30 years of academic and clinical experience, Dr. Lee has dedicated her career to ensuring that objective medical standards and empirical evidence — not bias, not financial incentive — determine the futures of children and families.

As an expert witness in more than 250 court cases — spanning family, civil, and criminal matters — across more than 30 states, Dr. Lee has witnessed firsthand the devastating consequences of unscientific forensic evaluations on families and children. She has described family court failures as one of the least recognized public health emergencies in the United States — a crisis that demands urgent national action, rigorous standards, and accountability.

Dr. Lee is the President of the World Mental Health Coalition and co-founder of Preventing Violence Now — organizations dedicated to advancing public mental health and preventing societal violence through research, education, and policy reform. She was the organizer of the landmark National Family Court Conference at the Washington Press Room, Washington D.C. — bringing together state legislators, legal experts, medical professionals, and families from across the country to advance a national dialogue on scientific rigor and evidence-based standards in family court.

Co-Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Rory Doyle

Healthcare Consultant | Entrepreneur | Executive Leader

Rory Doyle is a distinguished healthcare consultant, entrepreneur, and executive leader with more than 25 years of experience advising physicians, healthcare organizations, and emerging health-tech companies. He is deeply committed to advancing scientific accountability that protects families, promotes clinical precision and integrity, and safeguards public health.

Guided by a mission to elevate scientific excellence within legal systems, Mr. Doyle co-founded the Institute of Forensic Science — an organization dedicated to applying rigorous medical standards and evidence-based practices to family court proceedings throughout the United States and internationally.

Having partnered with more than 250 healthcare practices nationwide across neurosurgery, orthopedic surgery, interventional pain management, neurology, psychiatry and more — Mr. Doyle brings unparalleled operational expertise, strategic leadership, and a personal commitment to ensuring that no family faces family court complexity alone.

Together with Dr. Lee, Mr. Doyle is driving groundbreaking research on the national impact of current clinical, operational, and professional protocols — delivering actionable intelligence that empowers families, courts, and policymakers to make informed decisions based on empirical data, not guesswork.
