What is The Just Science Community?

The Just Science Community is the Institute of Forensic Science's ongoing resource platform — created specifically to eliminate the financial barriers that too many parents face when they cannot afford litigation or simply do not have access to the critical information they need to advocate for and protect their children.

Our mission is clear: ensuring every family is empowered with the critical insights and scientific excellence desperately needed in family courts across the country and beyond. Through The Just Science Community, you gain direct access to the expertise of Dr. Bandy Lee, M.D. and IOFS's groundbreaking research — on an ongoing monthly basis.

Frequently Asked Questions

Membership — What's Included

What does membership include?

+

Membership includes monthly one-hour educational sessions with Dr. Bandy Lee, M.D., advisory newsletters, forensic analysis, national data insights, and the ability to submit member questions across 15 categories of forensic expertise. All members also receive recording downloads of every session and full access to all past content — regardless of their ability to attend live. Members also have access to our growing content library — including past sessions, newsletters, and educational briefings available for individual purchase à la carte.

Who is The Just Science Community designed for?

+

The Just Science Community is designed for families, pro se litigants, legal professionals, policymakers, advocates, and guardians — anyone navigating the complexities of family court who needs access to credible, empirical, science-based expertise and actionable intelligence.

What if I can't attend the monthly session live?

+

All members have full access to all content and meetings regardless of their ability to attend live. Every session is recorded and made available as a downloadable file — so no meeting is ever missed.

How do I submit my questions?

+

Questions are reviewed for educational relevance and professional scope. Themes may inform future briefings or reports. Individual responses are not guaranteed, but all submissions are carefully reviewed and considered for inclusion in upcoming sessions and educational content.

What Membership Does Not Include

Does membership include legal advice?

+

No. IOFS does not provide legal advice or create an attorney–client relationship. All content is educational and policy-informed only and does not replace the advice of a licensed legal professional.

Does membership include therapy or diagnosis?

+

No. Membership does not provide treatment, diagnosis, or clinical care. All materials are for educational purposes only and do not create a doctor-patient relationship.

Is this the same as retaining Dr. Lee directly for my case?

+

No. The Just Science Community is a separate, accessible resource designed for ongoing education, advocacy, and support. If you need Dr. Lee to conduct a comprehensive forensic evaluation, peer review, or provide expert testimony in your specific case, please visit our Forensic Services page.
